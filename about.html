About the Ministry

The Mission of Repentance and Holiness — and the Call to Prepare

Our Mission

The Ministry of Repentance and Holiness exists to prepare the Church and the nations of the earth for the Glorious Coming of the Messiah, Jesus Christ.

Through the prophetic ministry of Prophet Dr. David Owuor, the LORD has spoken to the nations — calling all people to repentance, holiness, and readiness for the imminent return of Christ.

"A voice of one calling: 'In the wilderness prepare the way for the LORD; make straight in the desert a highway for our God.'"
— Isaiah 40:3

The Prophetic Ministry

Since the early 2000s, the ministry has documented over 150 prophetic words — including earthquakes, floods, political events, revivals, and healings — many of which have been independently verified and reported by major international media.

Global revival meetings have drawn millions of attendees across Africa, Europe, the Americas, and beyond, with documented miracles including healings of HIV/AIDS, blindness, and deafness confirmed by medical institutions.
